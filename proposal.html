The Tentative Project Title

The Status of HIV in NYC

Motivation for Project

HIV/AIDS have been a global health issue since the 1980s. Increase in health initiatives has raised awareness and provided better treatments for those suffering from these conditions. Though, much progress has been made, HIV/AIDS still pose a great threat to public health. This project will assess the current status of HIV/AIDS in NYC and attempt to find significant patterns within the data.

Intended Final Products

We intend to create visualizations that depict the current status of HIV in NYC. Additionally, we intend to assess the datasets to determine how much progress has been made over the years in terms of awareness and treatment. Ultimately, we hope to incorporate various forms of analyses including plots of distributions, regression analysis of possible factors that may influence the case rate, and a map using shiny to visualize the results that we have found.

The planned analyses / visualizations / coding challenges

The main challenge will be compiling the massive amounts of data and wrangling it to create a digestible report. We also expect to face some challenges in incorporating different datasets in a sensible way.
